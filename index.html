Frequently Asked Questions

Is AfriBuild available across all African countries?expand_more
Currently, we are fully operational in 8 major markets including Nigeria, Kenya, Ghana, South Africa, Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda, and Ethiopia, with plans to expand across the continent.
How do you verify suppliers?expand_more
Our multi-step verification includes business license checks, physical site visits, and mandatory escrow-based project history review.
What are the fees?expand_more
Browsing is free. We charge a small commission on successful transactions. Premium listings and subscriptions are available for suppliers and contractors.
How does escrow work?expand_more
Funds are held securely in escrow and released only when milestone deliverables are confirmed by both parties.
Can I list my property for free?expand_more
Yes, basic property listings are free. Premium placement and featured listings are available for a small fee.
Do you offer financing?expand_more
We partner with major banks and financial institutions to offer mortgage and construction loan options to qualified buyers.
How do I become a verified professional?expand_more
Apply through our directory. We verify your credentials, license, portfolio, and references before granting verified status.
What if I have a dispute?expand_more
Our dispute resolution team mediates all conflicts. Escrow-protected transactions have additional buyer protection guarantees.
